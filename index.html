Catmosphere café STHLM

This is no ordinary café, here you can enjoy a Swedish fika while spending time with our cats.

Catmosphere café STHLM established in 2020 in order to aid cat shelters financially. 5% of each purrchase is donated to the following cat shelters:

  • Stockholms katthem
  • Katthemmet i Vaxholm
  • Norrtälje kattcenter
  • Evas katthem

Our cats

Our cats are from various cat shelters. We care about the cats and because of this they retire after 3 months and go back the shelter in hope that they'll find their forever home.
